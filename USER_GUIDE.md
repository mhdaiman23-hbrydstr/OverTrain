## 🚀 Getting Started

### What is LiftLog?
LiftLog is a smart workout app that helps you:
- Track your gym workouts automatically
- Progress safely with intelligent weight increases
- Follow structured workout programs
- Analyze your strength gains over time
- Recover with built-in deload weeks

### Quick Setup
1. **Create Account** - Sign up with email and password
2. **Tell Us About Yourself** - Share your gender, experience level, and goals
3. **Choose a Program** - Select from beginner to advanced workout plans
4. **Start Working Out** - Begin your first training session

---

## 📱 How It Works

### Starting a Workout
1. Open the app and go to the "Workout" tab
2. You'll see today's exercises with suggested weights
3. Tap "Start Workout" to begin logging
4. For each exercise:
   - Enter the weight you used
   - Log how many reps you completed
   - Add notes if needed (optional)
5. Tap "Complete Workout" when finished

### Smart Progression System
LiftLog automatically suggests weight increases based on:

**Exercise Type Matters:**
- **Big Compound Exercises** (Squat, Deadlift): +5 lbs weekly
- **Medium Compound** (Bench Press, Rows): +2.5 lbs weekly  
- **Small Compound** (Pull-ups, Dips): +2.5 lbs weekly
- **Heavy Isolation** (Leg Extensions): +2.5 lbs weekly
- **Light Isolation** (Bicep Curls): +1 lb weekly

**How Progression Works:**
- Complete all reps with good form → Weight increases next week
- Miss reps → Same weight next week (try again)
- Miss reps twice → Weight reduced slightly for recovery

### Deload Weeks (Recovery)
Every 6th week is automatically a deload week:
- 20% lighter weights
- Fewer sets and reps
- Helps your body recover and get stronger
- You'll come back stronger in week 7

## 🎯 Key Features

### 1. Exercise Notes
- **Add Notes**: Tap the yellow banner above any exercise to add personal notes
- **Pin Notes**: Check "Pin this note" to carry it forward to future weeks
- **Auto-Cleanup**: Notes are automatically removed when exercises change

### 2. RPE & RIR Tracking
- **RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion)**: How hard the set felt (1-10 scale)
- **RIR (Reps in Reserve)**: How many more reps you could have done
- **Custom RPE**: Log RPE for each individual set
- **Block RPE/RIR**: See the intended intensity for each training block

### 3. Progress Analytics
- **Strength Charts**: Visualize your progress on each exercise
- **Volume Tracking**: See total weight lifted over time
- **Personal Records**: Automatic tracking of your best lifts
- **Workout History**: Complete log of all your training sessions

### 4. Mobile-First Design
- **Touch-Friendly**: Large buttons designed for gym use
- **Quick Entry**: Fast logging between sets
- **Offline Mode**: Works even without internet connection
- **Responsive**: Looks great on phones, tablets, and computers

## 🎯 Tips for Success

### For Best Results:
1. **Be Consistent** - Try to never miss scheduled workouts
2. **Log Accurately** - Record exactly what you did, every time
3. **Trust the Progression** - The system knows when to push and when to recover
4. **Use Deload Weeks** - Don't skip recovery weeks - they're essential
5. **Listen to Your Body** - The app guides you, but you know yourself best

### When to Adjust:
- **Too Easy?** - The system will automatically increase weights
- **Too Hard?** - The system will automatically reduce weights for recovery
- **Want Different Exercises?** - Choose a different program or create custom ones
- **Need More Rest?** - Take extra rest days, the program will adjust
